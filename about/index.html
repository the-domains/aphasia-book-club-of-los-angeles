Aphasia Book Club of Los Angeles / A place to read, share, and discuss the best of adult fiction and non-fiction

About

The purpose of our book club is to create an opportunity for persons with aphasia to read and discuss adult fiction and non-fiction books. Depending on reading ability, our members read books, listen to audiobook versions, or both.

We have two book club chapters. One chapter held at the Echo Park Branch Library every Thursday from 1:30 - 3:30 pm and another chapter held at the Mid-Valley Branch Library (Northridge) every Monday from 1:00 - 3:00 pm.
